All about it

Inception of "Bare Notes" happened during series of Jam sessions we guys used to do to pass our time over the weekends. Guitar, Synth and Djembe, those were the instruments in our jam and with melodious voice of Alankar, we always caught audience's attention.

But we wanted more. We wanted anyone and everyone to hear us play. So this thought of starting a Youtube channel to get more audience kinda stuck in our mind and there it was, "Bare Notes" . Armed with a Mac, Synth, Guitar and an iPhone we started our journey that will take us to places and eventually to people's heart.

The name "Bare Notes" itself defines us as a group. We want our songs to be simple in terms of music. Idea is to cover songs that we like in our own rendition. We will not limit ourselves to cover songs but will spread our wings and fly with our Original music as well.

We have dedicated our Weekends to this project as most of our time is demanded by our professional life. You can find us roaming around places to shoot videos when we are not recording music. We tried our hands in audio and video editing for the first time and hopefully we will get better at it one song at a time.
